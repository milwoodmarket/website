Vendor Info

Rules, Terms & Conditions

Please read all rules and terms carefully before submitting your vendor application. Vendors will be asked to acknowledge these terms as part of the application process.

General Information

  1. 1.By applying, vendors agree to abide by all Milwood Market rules, terms, and local regulations.
  2. 2.If required, vendors must have all necessary permits, licenses, and insurance available on-site for the duration of the market event.
  3. 3.Milwood Market reserves the right to revoke a vendor's approval at any time for rule violations, unsafe behavior, or failure to comply with policies.

Eligibility & Juried Approval

  1. 4.This event is juried to ensure a diverse and high-quality market. Applications must include a website, product photos, and/or social media handles to verify the uniqueness of items.
  2. 5.Milwood Market accepts vendors selling handcrafted goods, locally made products, or food items consistent with the market's standards.
  3. 6.Approval notifications will be sent via email. Submission of an application does not guarantee acceptance.

Booth Space & Setup

  1. 7.All booths are 10'×10' unless otherwise specified. Vendors must use space under tables for storage; all visible areas must be neat and free of clutter.
  2. 8.Vendors must provide all setup materials, including canopy, tables, chairs, signage, weights (minimum 40 lbs per canopy leg), and any equipment needed. Stakes in the ground are not allowed.
  3. 9.Setup begins 2 hours prior to the market. Vendors must be ready 30 minutes before opening unless prior arrangements are made.
  4. 10.Vendors must haul items to their designated space. The use of dollies or carts may be necessary, as some spaces may not be easily accessible by vehicle. Vehicles must be moved immediately after unloading.
  5. 11.Breakdown: Pack all items, including tent and canopy, before moving your vehicle for loading. Vendors are expected to remain for the full duration of the market. If you sell out, you may display a 'Sold Out' sign and return at the end of the market for teardown.

Conduct & Operations

  1. 12.No soliciting is allowed. Vendors may not yell, walk around distributing samples, or otherwise interrupt other vendors or visitors.
  2. 13.All signage must be neat, professional, and clearly indicate your booth type.
  3. 14.Vendors must display a valid tax ID certificate when/if required.
  4. 15.Vendors must bring a receptacle for their trash and dispose of it at teardown. Market trash bins are for public use only.

Fees, Payment & Cancellation

  1. 17.Booth fees are due in full within two weeks of approval. If payment is not received by that time, the space may be released to another vendor. For vendors approved less than two weeks before the market, the last day to make payment is the day before the event.
  2. 18.Fees are non-refundable, except in the case of market cancellation by the organizer.
  3. 19.No refunds will be issued for inclement weather or vendor cancellations. If Milwood Market cancels due to weather, the organizer will make every effort to schedule a makeup date that works with the vendor's schedule.
  4. 20.Vendors who cancel three times in a calendar year may lose priority for future markets and could be declined at the organizer's discretion.

Parking

  1. 20.Vendors must park in designated areas only. No parking is allowed in areas marked as restricted on vendor maps. Unauthorized vehicles may be towed at the owner's expense.
  2. 21.Vendor parking maps will be provided prior to the market.

Insurance & Liability

  1. 22.Vendors participating do so at their own risk and release Milwood Market, its organizers, and property owners from liability for accidents, injuries, theft, or property damage.
  2. 23.Vendors are responsible for any damage caused by their tents, equipment, or products.
  3. 24.Liability insurance is required for food vendors or other high-risk products.

Market Rights

  1. 25.Milwood Market reserves the right to refuse or remove any vendor who violates rules or local laws.
  2. 26.The organizer's decisions are final.
