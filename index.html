Our Story

A market rooted in
community

Milwood Market was born from a simple idea: that neighbors deserve a place to gather, discover, and celebrate the incredible talent and craft right in their own backyard. We're a neighborhood market dedicated to showcasing local makers, food vendors, artisans, and small businesses.

Our vendors are creators and artisans from across the greater community — each bringing something unique and handcrafted to share. From hand-thrown pottery to freshly baked bread, from artisanal foods to handmade jewelry — everything here has a story, and every story is local.

We believe markets are more than shopping. They're where friendships start, where kids discover something new, and where makers find their audience. Come hungry, come curious, and come ready to connect.

Visitor Information

Everyone is welcome at Milwood Market — admission is always free! Leashed pets, strollers, and wagons are welcome. A fenced-in area with tables and shade is available for relaxing. We are located in the Milwood Shopping Center parking lot, adjacent to the community garden and Milwood Library. Come ready to enjoy handcrafted goods, fresh food, live music, and more.
